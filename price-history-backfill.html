💰 Price History Backfill

Not connected
One-time backfill. Scans the work-order Drive folders (2 - Ready to Invoice and 3 - Invoice Sent) and seeds the Price History tab that powers the pricing panel on the work order form. Each WO is read from the best source available: JSON full form data · DOC parsed from the Google Doc text · PROPS Drive metadata only (older files stored just the first service, so these are flagged ⚠ may be incomplete and left unchecked — open the file, verify, then tick to include). WOs already in the Price History tab are skipped, so re-running is safe. Nothing is written until you click Import.
