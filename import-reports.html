📥 Import External Reports

Not connected
Searches Gmail for inspection reports from outside vendors (Convergint, Martinez Fire), reads each PDF to guess property / system / date, and (on your OK) uploads them to FLPS Software › FLPS Inspection Reports named EXT_… so they show on the audit page. Invoices are auto-flagged and skipped. Nothing is uploaded until you click Import. After importing, the email is tagged with the Gmail label Inspect_Rpt_Imported, and the default search excludes that label so handled emails won't come back.
ⓘ How the matching works & what it costs
Matching: With Auto-read ON, Claude reads each PDF and returns whether it's an inspection report (vs. invoice), the vendor, property, system (sprinkler, FDC hydrostatic, internal pipe, backflow, …) and inspection date. The property is matched against your client list; the system + date drive the filename so the audit page slots it into the right row. You can override any guess in the dropdowns before importing. With Auto-read OFF, those fields come from the email subject/filename only — free, but rougher.

Cost: Only Claude costs money (Google + Drive are free), and only on report PDFs (invoices are skipped). Each PDF is read once and cached; handled emails are excluded from later searches, so you don't re-pay. Rough per-report estimate by model:
