📖NFPA 10 References & Inspection Procedure
NFPA 10 – 2010: §7.1.2, 7.2.2, 7.2.4, 7.3.1  |  Frequency: Not more than one year, at time of hydrostatic test, or when indicated by inspection.

Each unit shall be checked for: Date of last service · Designated location & no obstruction · Proper height & bracket/cabinet · Cabinet components (glass, mallet, key locks) · Legible nameplate instructions facing outward · Safety seals & tamper indicators intact · Fullness by weight/"hefting" · Physical damage, corrosion, leakage, clogged nozzle · Pressure gauge in operable range · Tires/wheels/carriage/hose/nozzle (wheeled units)
