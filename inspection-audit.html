How to read this: Each inspection on record (from the Inspection History sheet — the same data as the Inspection Schedule page) is matched to saved PDFs in FLPS Software › FLPS Inspection Reports by property + system. A system can have several reports (e.g. multiple kitchen hoods) — all are listed on that one record, with their dates. A missing flag means there's a record but no PDF in Drive — locate or re-upload it. For missing rows we also point at the likely 🔎 Work Log line (and the invoice it was billed under) to help you track the report down. A report tagged not in inspection schedule means the PDF exists but no inspection was ever logged for it — worth adding to the schedule. Backflow and 5-year tests (FDC hydrostatic, internal pipe) are often done by outside vendors (Morrison / Martinez / Convergint) — import their PDFs via Import External Reports and they'll match here like any other report. 3- and 5-year cycles only show a "next due" when a baseline date exists. Matches tagged (likely) are best-guess — open to confirm.
